ngx.config.debug
----------------
**语法:** *debug = ngx.config.debug*

**环境:** *set_by_lua*, rewrite_by_lua*, access_by_lua*, content_by_lua*, header_filter_by_lua*, body_filter_by_lua*, log_by_lua*, ngx.timer.*, init_by_lua*, init_worker_by_lua**
